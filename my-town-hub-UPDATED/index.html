Why It Matters

Charlotte is growing fast.
Our neighbors need support.

How QueenCityConnect Works

Three simple steps to get support

A quick workflow designed for residents, families, and case managers.

01

Search and Filter

Use keyword search and category filters to narrow the resource hub to your exact need.

02

Review Verified Details

Open resource cards to check phone numbers, addresses, hours, and official organization websites.

03

Connect Right Away

Call, visit, or share the resource immediately. Use the submit page to suggest more local resources.

Data Quality

How we verify each resource

We use a repeatable checklist so residents can trust the information in this directory.

Official Source Check

Each listing is cross-checked against an official organization website or county service page before publishing.

Contact Detail Review

We verify address, phone, service category, operating hours, and active website links for every resource card.

Rolling Updates

Community submissions are reviewed before inclusion, and listings are re-audited on a regular cycle for accuracy.
