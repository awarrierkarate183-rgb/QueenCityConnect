Our Mission

Connecting Charlotte residents to the help they need

Charlotte is one of the fastest-growing cities in the US, but that growth brings real challenges. Thousands of residents struggle to find food, housing, mental health care, and other essential services.

QueenCityConnect is a free, searchable directory of local nonprofits and community programs, all in one place, available 24/7.
