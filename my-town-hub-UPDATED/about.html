About QueenCityConnect

Why we built this, who it's for, and the community data behind it.

Our Mission

Connecting Charlotte residents to the help they need

Charlotte is one of the fastest-growing cities in the United States — but rapid growth brings real challenges. Thousands of Mecklenburg County residents struggle to find food, stable housing, mental health care, and other essential services. Many don't know where to start.

QueenCityConnect was created to solve that problem. We've compiled a comprehensive, easy-to-search directory of local nonprofits, community programs, and support services — all in one place, free to use, available 24/7.

Whether you're a resident seeking help, a social worker looking for referrals, or a community member who wants to give back — this hub is for you.

Who This Hub Serves

QueenCityConnect is built for every member of the Charlotte community.

Residents in Need

Find food banks, shelters, free clinics, mental health support, and more — without navigating dozens of separate websites. We put it all in one place.

Social Workers & Case Managers

Quickly look up and share verified resources with clients. Our print feature makes it easy to hand a resource card directly to someone who needs it.

Teachers & School Counselors

Connect students and families to community support services — from tutoring programs to emergency food assistance and mental health counseling.

Local Organizations

Get listed in front of thousands of Charlotte residents by submitting your organization through our easy, free submission form.
